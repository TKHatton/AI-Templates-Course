Decision-Making Frameworks

The 4-Component Template Framework

1. Context: Define your role and situation
2. Purpose: Clarify your objective
3. Structure: Specify output format
4. Parameters: Provide specific data

Decision-First Approach

1. Identify Decision: What needs to be decided?
2. Gather Information: What data is needed?
3. Analyze Options: What are the alternatives?
4. Make Choice: Select the best option
