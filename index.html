Resource Guide

Comprehensive reference materials, key concepts, and professional templates from the course.

1

Actionable Information

Decision-First Approach: Start with the specific decision you need to make, then gather the exact information required for that decision.

Three Elements: Specific numbers/details, Context/comparison, Clear next steps

2

Template Components

Four Essential Parts: Context (your role/situation), Purpose (what you want to achieve), Structure (output format), Parameters (specific data/constraints)

Testing: Use 3-5 times for consistency, test with different data for adaptability

3

Core Components

Structured Output: Organized, consistent formatting that's easy to scan and act upon

Action Frameworks: Clear next steps with specific timelines and responsible parties

Business Performance Template

CONTEXT: I am a small business owner analyzing monthly performance metrics for strategic decision-making.

PURPOSE: I need to evaluate our business performance to identify growth opportunities and areas requiring immediate attention.

STRUCTURE: Please organize the analysis into: 1) Revenue Analysis 2) KPI Summary 3) Top 3 Strengths and Opportunities 4) Action Items with Timeline

PARAMETERS: Monthly Revenue: $[amount], Key Metrics: [list], Industry: [type], Team Size: [number]

Decision-First Framework

  1. What specific decision do I need to make?
  2. What information is required for this decision?
  3. How will I measure success?
  4. What are the next steps?

Template Testing Framework

  1. Consistency Test: Use 3-5 times with same data
  2. Adaptability Test: Use with different data sets
  3. Quality Check: Verify all four components present
  4. Results Review: Evaluate output usefulness
